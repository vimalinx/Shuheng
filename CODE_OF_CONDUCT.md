# Code of Conduct

## Standards

Project discussions should stay technical, specific, and respectful. Good
participation includes:

- Reporting issues with concrete reproduction steps.
- Disagreeing through evidence and trade-off analysis.
- Respecting security disclosure boundaries.
- Keeping release claims accurate and not overstating experimental surfaces.

Unacceptable participation includes harassment, threats, doxxing, knowingly
publishing private secrets, or derailing technical discussion with personal
attacks.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may hide, edit, or remove comments, issues, pull requests, or other
contributions that violate these standards. Repeated or severe violations may
lead to being blocked from the project.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainer. Include links, screenshots,
and context where possible.
